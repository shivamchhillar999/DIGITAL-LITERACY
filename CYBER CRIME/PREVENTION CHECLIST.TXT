Stay Safe Online: Checklist for College Students (India)
 General Online Safety
Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking or logging into important accounts.
Verify links before clicking – check the sender and URL carefully to avoid phishing scams.
Limit personal information sharing on social media (address, phone number, college details, etc.).
Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores and check reviews before installing.
Log out from shared or public devices after accessing your accounts.

Financial / UPI Safety
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps; receiving money never requires entering your PIN.
Double-check QR codes before scanning—fraudsters may send fake ones to steal money.
Enable transaction alerts on your bank account to monitor suspicious activity instantly.

 If Something Goes Wrong
Report fraud immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible to increase chances of recovering money
